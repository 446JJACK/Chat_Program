Here you will find additional information about JJACK's Chatroom. Consider this the ultimate F.A.Q page.

Website Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at JJACK is required to receive all benefits JJACK's Chatroom has to offer. The only requirement is a valid plattsburgh.edu email address with access to it in order to validate your account registration.

How do I activate my JJACK account?

After registering, login to your plattsburgh.edu email address. Please allow up to 10 minutes for your validation e-mail from us. Inside, you will see a activation URL, click that and you are all set!

How do I Live Chat?

Click Live Chat up above after logging in and validating your account to get started!

Chatroom Questions

What is my Display Name?

Your display name will be your plattsburgh email address without the numbers for security reasons. Example: jdoe9@plattsburgh.edu display name will be jdoe.

Can I send URL's?

Yes you may, however, some URL's may be censored and you are not permitted to link direct downloads other than to safe PDF's.

What is a Channel?

A channel is a sub-chatroom within the main lobby of JJACK's chatroom. By default, we offer pre-created department specific channels. Any public created chatroom will also appear on the list while it's active. To join a private chatroom, you must be invited by the owner.

What is the Active User List?

On the right side you will see the "Active Online List", these are students that have last been active in the past 10 minutes. If 10 minutes past, you will be logged off the chatroom but still logged into JJACK. Please refresh your page to join the chatroom again!

Can we discuss Homework?

As long as your discussion is legal and does not break any rules set forth by any professor, any discussion is acceptable.

How do I become a Chat Moderator?

At this time we are not looking for extra Moderator's.

Chatroom Functionality

How do I Private Message a student?

Click on a student's name from the "Active User List", from there, you will be able to private message the student.

How do I Block a student?

Click on a student's name from the "Active User List", from there, you will be able to block the student.

How do I Invite a student?

Click on a student's name from the "Active User List", from there, click 'invite' and then select which channel you wish to invite that student to.

How do I create a Channel?

Click on the + sign to the right of the currently displayed channels above, then you can name your channel and set it to either "Public" or "Private".
